1. A RECOMMENDATION, By the Reverend Mr. MICHAEL BOSTON, The Author's Grandſon:

1. PREFACE.

#####Body#####

1. STATE I. NAMELY, The State of INNOCENCE, or Primitive Integrity, in which Man was created.

    _ DOCTRINE, GOD made Man altogether righteous.

    _ Of MAN's Original Righteouſneſs.

    _ Of MAN'S Original Happineſs.

1. STATE II. NAMELY, The State of Nature, or of Entire Depravation.

    _ HEAD I. The Sinfulneſs of Man's Natural State.

      * That Man's Nature is corrupted.

      * Of the Corruption of the Underſtanding.

      * Of the Corruption of the Will.

      * The Corruption of the Affections, the Conſcience and the Memory. The Body partaker of this corruption.

      * How Man's Nature was corrupted.

      * The Doctrine of the Corruption of Nature applied.

    _ HEAD II. The MISERY of MAN's Natural State.

      * Man's natural State, a State of Wrath.

      * The Doctrine of the State of Wrath confirmed and vindicated.

      * The Doctrine of the Miſery of Man's natural State applied.

    _ HEAD III. Man's utter Inability to recover himſelf.

1. STATE III. NAMELY, The STATE of GRACE; or Begun Recovery.

    _ HEAD I. REGENERATION.

      * Of the Nature of Regeneration.

      * The Reſemblance 〈…〉.

      * The Doctrine of Regeneration applied.

    _ HEAD II. The MYSTICAL UNION betwixt CHRIST and Believers.

      * A general View of the Myſtical Union.

      * Of the natural and ſupernatural Stocks, and the Branches, taken out of the former, and ingrafted into the latter.

      * How the •ranches are taken out of the natural Stock, and ingrafted into the ſupernatural Stock.

      * Of the Benefits flowing to true believers, from th•ir union with Chriſt.

1. STATE IV. NAMELY, The ETERNAL STATE: or, State of conſummate Happineſs, or Miſery.

    _ HEAD I. Of DEATH.

      * DOCTRINE, All muſt die.

    _ HEAD II. The Difference betwixt the Righteous and the Wicked in their Death.

      * DOCTRINE I. The Wicked dying, are driven away in their Wickedneſs, and in a hopeleſs State.

      * DOCTRINE II. The State of the Godly in Death, is a hopeful State.

    _ HEAD III The RESURRECTION.

      * DOCTRINE. There ſhall be a Reſurrection of the Dead.

    _ HEAD IV. The General JUDGMENT.

      * DOCTRINE. There ſhall be a general Judgment.

    _ HEAD V. The Kingdom of HEAVEN.

      * DOCTRINE, The Saints ſhall be made compleatly happy in the Poſſeſſion of the Kingdom of Heaven.

    _ HEAD VI. HELL.

      * DOCTRINE. The wicked ſhall be ſhut up under the curſe of God, in everlaſting Miſery, with the Devils in Hell.
